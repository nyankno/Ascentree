In the Beginning...

To him who is determined to make a difference, the wherewithal is always available.


Once known as ACC-SPECS Professional Services Ltd., our journey took a transformative turn in 2021. With a renewed vision and a new name, ASCENTREE emerged to address a crucial gap we identified in the business solutions space. Our evolution wasn't just about rebranding; it was about recognizing the broader needs of organizations striving to thrive in a fiercely competitive marketplace.


From our inception, we've grown and matured, expanding our capabilities to offer a comprehensive suite of services. We understand that success demands more than just technology; it requires a holistic approach. That's why ASCENTREE is dedicated to helping businesses not only meet their statutory obligations to stakeholders but also to innovate and excel.


At ASCENTREE, our transformative approach turns businesses into structured organizations with robust systems, processes, and policies designed for sustained growth. We’ve guided countless clients through challenging growth phases, helping them adopt new practices that ensure lasting success. Our clients often see us as loyal partners, walking alongside them on their journey towards enduring prosperity.


At ASCENTREE, our technology solutions are built on strong partnerships with renowned global providers such as Microsoft, SAP, and QuickBooks. Over the years, we've amassed extensive experience by delivering high quality solutions and successfully completing projects for clients across diverse sectors of the Nigerian economy. Our team of consultants is not only well-trained but also certified, ensuring we provide the best business solutions. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted partner for organizations looking to harness the power of technology for their growth and success.
