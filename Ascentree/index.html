ABOUT US

Africa’s Leading Technology and Business Consultancy Firm.

Once known as ACC-SPECS Professional Services Ltd., our journey took a transformative turn in 2021. With a renewed vision and a new name, ASCENTREE emerged to address a crucial gap we identified in the business solutions space.

Our evolution wasn't just about rebranding; it was about recognizing the broader needs of organizations striving to thrive in a fiercely competitive marketplace.
